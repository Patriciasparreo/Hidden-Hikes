Welcome to Hidden Hikes!

  • Why hidden...?

    You won't find crowds of people at any of our hidden hikes. Depending on the day, you and your party might be alone on the trails, giving the illusion that you are truly the first to discover this particular gem. Witnessing silence and solitude while in such beautiful surroundings is an experience that we feel you simply can't get anywhere else. Yet, as peaceful as they are, these hikes also evoke a thrilling and most satisfying sense of adventure in that you feel like the only hiker to have ever traveled the trail.

    The hikes are lesser-known and are often easy to miss even when you are actively looking for them. They feature short trips similar to those found in state and county parks, but do not require admission charges or parking fees. With that said, keep in mind that they also tend to lack some of the basic amenities. For instance, most of them do not have restroom facilities. This makes them most suitable for short treks (lasting only a half-day or less), or as awesome, quick diversions when you are enroute to some other destination. Sound intriguing...? Then go exploring today!

  • Wondering what to expect...?

    You've come to the right place! We've collected all the pertinent information that you'll need to know before you visit each hiking location. The locations map will give you some perspective as to where each hike can be found, and also what you may be interested in around that area.

    To check out the details for each adventure, click on the name of the specific site from the menu above. Here, we describe the type of hike, the amenities (like parking availability), and what you should expect to find there when you arrive. Each site also includes a photo gallery so that you can get a sneak peek of the locale without ever leaving home!

    You'll find a wide variety to choose from, including an urban oasis tucked away just out of sight, trails along a gulch near the neighboring Bigfoot Discovery Museum, a redwood forest teeming with native flora and fauna, and a secret cove that boasts both beach and cliff trails. We hope that we've piqued your curiosity and provided you with everything you need to keep on adventuring!

  • We'd love to hear from you!

    We are always looking for new hidden gems to add to our site. We don't have any hard and fast rules about what is required to be considered a candidate for inclusion here on Hidden Hikes. If you have a favorite that you think we should know about, please use the link below to tell us all the details.
